## Quick FAQ (Top 5 Questions)

**What is JeuXP?**  
JeuXP is a challenge center: you enter, play for 3 minutes per challenge, then step out! Your goal: beat your score, earn XP points, and reach all 5 levels.

**Do I need to book in advance?**  
No reservation is required. Just show up whenever you want and play!

**How many people can play?**  
You can play solo or in a team of 2 to 4 players.

**What's the minimum age?**  
Access is not permitted for children under 6 years old. Players under 12 years old must be accompanied by an adult. We recommend ages 16 and up to fully enjoy the experience.

**How long does a challenge last?**  
Each challenge lasts 3 minutes. You can play as many rounds as you want, depending on the time package you've chosen.
